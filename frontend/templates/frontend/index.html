Meet Bello.

Bello is a 59-year-old Nigerian migrant who was SOLD and is a survivor of the modern slavery and human trafficking happening in Libya.

A Father of 6 children, he risked his life crossing deadly seas to seek greener pastures in Europe and could easily have become one of over 10,000 refugees (Amnesty International, 2017) who have perished at sea making this lethal voyage.

He’s made his way back to Ghana and, with Joseph Awah-Darko’s support, now seeks to use sports to share his experience and create awareness to end the sale, trade and commodification of Africans. This is to fulfil the UN Sustainable Development Goal of a ‘Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions’.
