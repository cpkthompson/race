Bello and Joseph Awuah-Darko, will run nearly 125 from Accra to Kumasi to raise awareess and funds for Amnesty International in their legal battle against complicit European Governments involved in the violation of human rights regarding Libyan refugees and migrants. We are calling this journey #TheRaceForHumanDignity. But first we seek to get Bello prosthetic aid to run and we would appreciate all the support we could get!

In the same vein, Joseph Awuah-Darko himself will be representing Ghana in the 2020 Olympics [long distance: 5,000m/10,000m] to raise human rights awareness towards the emancipation of those held captive in Libya.

“The commodification and enslavement of Africans in the 21st Century is completed inexcusable and must end!”
